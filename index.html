Palash Shah




Hi i'm Palash Shah! I'm currently a sophomore at the University of Virginia studying computer science and commerce. I love bringing unorthodox ideas to life with software.

I recently authored Libra: a machine learning API that lets you build and train models in just one-line of code. We currently have over 9,400 users, and more then 1850 stars on GitHub. You can find the documentation site here.

I'm also working with Dr. Menon from Carnegie Mellon University, on using CycleGANS for a novel way to utilize 3-D Lung NII images for early detection. More information about this will be coming soon.

This summer, I worked at Leidos as a part of the GeoAxis group. I helped with the design of a new access-portal used by government customers. Before that, I led a team of researchers at Fondazione Bruno Kesseler in Trentino Italy to help with brain tumor identification and segmentation.

I'm excited about reinforcement learning as well as scalable distributed systems. Please reach out to me if you're interested in chatting about Google's Duality framework or Actor Critics.

I'm also interested in computer science education. I've served as a Teaching Assistant for UVA's CS 2102 (Discrete Mathematics) and now for CS 4774 (Machine Learning).
